About The Project

The Mediterranean region is warming at a rate around twenty percent faster than the global average, leading to an increased frequency of extreme weather events like droughts, floods, and water scarcity. The INCREASE4MED project aims to deliver an innovative and scalable framework for sustainable water and land management by integrating regionally tailored Nature-based Solutions (NbS) in pilot areas across six Mediterranean countries: Türkiye, Greece, Italy, Jordan, Morocco, and Spain. The project weaves together ecological, socio-economic, and governance aspects to build resilience across the Mediterranean.
